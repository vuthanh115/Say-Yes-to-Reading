Bedtime

National Geographic Kids 🌙

The sun goes down, and the moon comes up. It's time for bedtime! 🌙

An orangutan napps in a cozy nest high up in the trees. 🌳

A lion cuddles with its family for a long snooze in the grass. 🦁

A sea otter wraps itself in seaweed so it doesn't float away. 🦦

Splash! It's time for a warm bath with lots of bubbles. 🛁

Put on your soft pajamas. They feel so snug and warm! 👕

Brush, brush, brush your teeth to keep them white and clean. 🪥

Settle in and listen to a wonderful bedtime story. 📖

Give a big hug and a goodnight kiss to your mommy and daddy. 💋

Turn off the light. The room is quiet and dark. 💡

Close your eyes and have sweet dreams. Goodnight! ✨
