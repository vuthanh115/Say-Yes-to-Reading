Ocean

National Geographic Kids 🌊

The ocean is a big, blue place. Let's dive in! 🌊

A blue whale is the biggest animal on Earth. It is huge! 🐋

Playful dolphins jump out of the waves. Splash! 🐬

A sea turtle swims with its flippers. It goes to the sandy beach. 🐢

A shark has many sharp teeth and a fin on its back. 🦈

An octopus has eight long arms and can hide in the rocks. 🐙

Bright orange clownfish hide in the wavy sea anemone. 🐠

A crab scuttles sideways on the ocean floor. Click, click! 🦀

A tiny seahorse holds onto seaweed with its tail. 🐎

A starfish has five points. It looks like a star in the sea! ⭐

The ocean is full of amazing animals. Which one is your favorite?
