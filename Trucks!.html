Trucks!

National Geographic Kids 🚛

Some trucks are big. Some trucks are small. Let's see them all! 🚛

The fire truck races to help people and squirts water with its hose. Wee-woo! 🚒

Rocks and dirt tumble out of the big dump truck. Rumble, rumble! 🏗️

The concrete truck helps build a sidewalk with wet concrete. 🏠

The garbage truck rumbles and clanks as it picks up the trash. 🗑️

The snowplow shovels deep snow off the cold road. ❄️

The pickup truck delivers fresh food from the farm to you. 🚜

The tanker truck carries food, fuel, or water in its big tank. ⛽

A tow truck uses a cable to pull a broken car for a ride. 🪝

An ice cream truck plays a happy tune. It keeps the treats cold! 🍦

Trucks do many important jobs. Which truck do you like best?
