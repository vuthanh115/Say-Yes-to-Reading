Animals on the Go

National Geographic Kids 🌍

Animals move in many ways. Let's see how they go! 🌍

A kangaroo hops across the grass. Hop, hop, hop! 🦘

A cheetah runs very fast on four legs. Look at it go! 🐆

A dolphin jumps high out of the water. Splash! 🐬

A snake slithers across the rocks. It has no legs at all! 🐍

An orangutan swings from branch to branch in the trees. 🦧

An ostrich walks on two very long legs. Step, step, step! 🪶

A ladybug crawls over a beautiful flower. 🐞

A black bear climbs up a tall tree. Up it goes! 🐻

A penguin slides on its belly across the cold ice. Whee! 🐧

Many animals move in many ways. How do you move?
