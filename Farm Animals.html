Farm Animals

National Geographic Kids 🚜

Hello, farm! There are many amazing animals here. 🐄

Moo! Cows eat green grass. They give us cold milk in a glass. 🥛

Cluck, cluck! Hens lay eggs, and tiny chicks hatch from them. 🐣

Baa! Sheep grow soft wool. We use their wool to make warm sweaters. 🧶

Neigh! Horses run very fast. They can even sleep standing up! 🐎

Oink, oink! Baby pigs are called piglets. They use their snouts to find food. 🐷

Quack! Ducks love to swim in the cool pond with their webbed feet. 🦆

A goat has horns and likes to climb. It eats almost anything! 🐐

Hee-haw! A donkey is strong and can carry heavy things on the farm. 🫏

Woof! The farm dog barks to help the farmer. It is a good friend. 🐕

Which farm animal is your favorite? Goodnight, farm!
